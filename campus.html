Additional Facilities at YWMS

YWMS provides holistic education beyond textbooks, nurturing intellectual, physical, emotional, and social development.

Extensive Library

Well-organized collection in English & Tamil to foster curiosity and reading habits.

Well-Equipped Labs

Physics, Chemistry, Biology & Computer Science labs with modern equipment.

Martial Arts

Karate & Silambam to build discipline, confidence & fitness.

Sports & Games

Indoor & outdoor activities including Athletics, Volleyball, Kabaddi, Chess.

Competitive Sports

Participation in Zonal, District & State-level competitions with accolades.

Cultural Events

Dance, music, drama, traditional arts nurturing creativity & stage confidence.

Leadership & Service

Scout, NSS, JRC programs promoting discipline, civic sense & leadership.

Abacus & Speed Math

Mental arithmetic & focus improvement for young learners.

Clean Drinking Water

Purified water facilities ensuring safety and hygiene across campus.

Large Playground

Spacious grounds for sports, physical training, and recreational activities.

Olympiad Exams

Unified International Olympiad for Grades III–XII to challenge and excel globally.

Literary Club

Reading, writing, debates, elocution, and creative expression for students.

Thulir Science Talent Exam

Encouraging analytical thinking & STEM aptitude for Grades VI–XI.

About Us

In 1987, inspired by the visit of former President of India Dr. Zakir Husain to the village mosque inauguration years earlier, the Forum, with the generous support of Alhaj E.S.M. Bakker Mohamed, established the Dr. Zakir Husain English School. It began humbly with just 26 students in LKG and UKG.
